Mercedes-AMG GLC 63 4MATIC+

With the AMG 4.0 liter V8 biturbo engine with its 350 kW (476 PS) and 650 Nm of the Mercedes-AMG GLC 63 4MATIC+ Coupé, you can enjoy a completely new experience of your everyday life. Powerful and impressive at first sight this is a vehicle that will make lap times a part of everyday life. The A-shaped AMG-specific radiator grill corresponds fully to the vehicle’s driving performance fit for a racetrack. A performance that, thanks to the adjustable sports chassis, you can enjoy even off the circuit.

The two chrome-plated double end pipe panels of the exhaust systems complete the highly-dynamic line design as a whole. From them rumbles the unmistakable sound of the optional switchable AMG Performance exhaust system. The motivation is reflected in the interior as well, which is precisely focused on the driver: Features from the racetrack like the AMG steering wheel buttons enable a fast and purposeful operation of the driving-dynamics settings – for a driving experience that leaves behind the everyday life, yet makes it the everyday.
