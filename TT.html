Audi TTrs

The engine is a four-cylinder EA888 turbocharged petrol unit. Here it makes 306bhp between 5800 and 6200rpm, and 280lb ft, which arrives at only 1800rpm, and sticks gamely around until 100rpm before the arrival of peak power. Plump, then. To put it in perspective the TT RS uses the same five-cylinder, 2.5-litre unit bestowed under the bonnet of the departed RS3 with the wick turned up slightly.
